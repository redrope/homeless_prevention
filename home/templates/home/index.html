Emergency Financial Assistance (English/Spanish)

The purpose of this program is to provide financial assistance to low income renters/homeowners in Maricopa County faced with a financial crisis and threat of eviction. If you are facing a temporary financial hardship Society of St. Vincent de Paul may be able to help.

Steps you need to take to request assistance.
  1. Gather documents – ID, lease, pay loss/reduction, utility bills (see required document list)
  2. Make sure your lease or mortgage documents have your signature and the landlord’s signature
  3. Scan or take photos of documentation
  4. Calculate your total household income
  5. Get addresses and phone number of your landlord or housing company
  6. Make sure the person named on the lease is the person listed on the application
Start your Assistance Application ** IMPORTANT **

Before you click the Start Application button below. Please have the following information ready in a digital format (see FAQ’s for digital upload information and full list of documentation):

Documents and information, you must provide for the application to be considered

See the FAQ document for more information on types of documents that can be submitted.

You will need to upload a scanned image or photo image of each of the following:
  • Identification for the applicant: Driver's license, State issued ID, Social Security Card, Voter Registration. This ID must be the person on the rental agreement.
  • Hardship supporting documentation: Employment termination letter or employer letter stating wage reduction.
  • Rent or Mortgage Lease agreement: Current unaltered lease or mortgage agreement with signatures.
  • Utility bills: Current utility bills with account number, address, and amount owed.
  • Optional document: Proof of citizenship
Other information you will need for the Application
  • Landlord’s name, address and telephone number
  • Household gross income for the last 30 days
  • A username and password that is 8 letters long and includes capital letters and numbers
To be considered for financial assistance complete an online application.
